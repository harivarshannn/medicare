RISEN PsyLabs is a behavioral science–led consulting firm specializing in psychometric assessments and capability diagnostics that enable organizations to make evidence-based decisions.

Overview

Our assessments provide deep insights into leadership potential, mental health parameters, and workforce well-being, forming a strong diagnostic foundation for talent and coaching decisions. The insights generated through our psychometric evaluations are translated into precise, Training Need Identification (TNI) metrics, ensuring that learning interventions are targeted, relevant, and outcome-driven. We further support organizations through competency framework development, structured coaching models, and rigorous evaluation processes—ensuring every development initiative is measurable, defensible, and aligned with individual growth.

Our Approach

Why Risen PsyLabs

We bring together rigorous psychological expertise, market-leading assessment technologies, and a distinctly human-led approach. We move beyond standardized tools to design context-specific assessments that reflect real workplace and psychological behaviors.

This enables greater precision in identifying potential, role alignment, mental well-being, and coaching roadmap pathways—making our assessments not only scientifically robust, but also meaningful for organizational decisions and desirable for individual career growth.
